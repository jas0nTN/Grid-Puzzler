Change the template by inputing different values, definitions, or words of your choice to make a 3x3 word puzzle. The inputs are correspondants to the template's values.

Step 1

Choose a certain question, answer, or dummy question you want to change on the board

Step 2

Type the value, definition, word, or anything you would like it to be!

Step 3

Fill the rest of the form boxes out and click on the execute button to see the changes you've made to the puzzle.

Step 4

You are almost done! Now just hit "CTRL" + "P" to print out the new puzzle you have just created. Congratulations!
